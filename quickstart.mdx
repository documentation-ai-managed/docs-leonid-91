## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid email address for registration
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- Optional: A custom domain ready for later connection

<Callout kind="info">
Leonid 91 works best on desktop browsers for the initial setup. Mobile optimization comes after launch.
</Callout>

## Sign Up and Access Your Dashboard

Follow these steps to create your account and log in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.leonid91.com/register`.

    Fill in your email, create a strong password, and agree to the terms.

    Click **Create Account**. You'll receive a verification email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Open the email from Leonid 91 and click the verification link.

    This activates your account instantly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Go to `https://app.leonid91.com/login`.

    Enter your email and password, then click **Sign In**.

    You'll land on the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Website

Build and launch a basic site in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Template" icon="magic-wand">
    From the dashboard, select **New Site**.

    Pick a template like "Business Starter" for agencies.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Edit the homepage: Replace placeholder text with your business details.

    Add pages via **+ Page** > "About" or "Services".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview and Launch" icon="rocket">
    Click **Preview** to test.

    Hit **Publish** to go live at a Leonid 91 subdomain like `yourbusiness.leonid91.app`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Customization Options

Tailor your site without coding.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Theme" icon="palette">
    Go to **Design** > **Theme**.

    Select colors (brand: `#7c7c7c`), fonts, and layouts.

    Changes apply site-wide instantly.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Content Blocks" icon="blocks">
    Drag-and-drop blocks in the editor:

    - Hero section for headlines
    - Testimonials for client quotes
    - Contact form for leads
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="SEO Settings" icon="search">
    In **Settings** > **SEO**:

    Add meta title: "Fast Websites by Agenc.io"

    Description: "Build search-friendly sites with Leonid 91."

    Enable auto-sitemap generation.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Connect a custom domain later via **Settings** > **Domains**. Point your DNS A record to Leonid 91's IP.
</Callout>
