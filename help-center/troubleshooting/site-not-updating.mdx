---
title: Site Not Updating After Changes
description: Resolve website changes not appearing online.
---

## Symptoms
- Updates don’t show up on live site
- Cache shows previous version

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Force Refresh">
    ````text
    Hold Shift + click reload in your browser.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache">
    Log into Leonid 91 Dashboard → Settings → Clear Site Cache
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Publish Status">
    Confirm you clicked "Publish" after editing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
