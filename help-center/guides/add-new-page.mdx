---
title: Add a New Page in Leonid 91
description: Quick tutorial on creating a page for your site.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Log into agenc.io and go to your Leonid 91 site's dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Pages">
    Click "Pages" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Page">
    Click "Add Page", name it, and choose a layout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Add your content and hit "Publish" to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
